Questions buyers ask

Clear answers for small-business decision makers.

Can we use our existing SOP documents?

Yes. Existing SOPs can be brought into the portal and organized into searchable procedures. AI-assisted drafting can help structure content, but managers still review before publishing.

Do staff need to install an app?

The portal is mobile-friendly in a browser. QR codes can open SOP links on phones and tablets, making procedures portable without forcing staff back to a desk.

How does this help training?

Managers can create training questions, review completion, use read confirmations, and keep important instructions next to the procedure itself.

Can staff suggest changes?

Yes. Staff can submit change requests when a process is unclear, outdated, or missing. Managers review and approve updates before they become the current version.

Is it branded for each business?

Yes. The portal can use the customer’s company name, portal name, colors, logo, support details, and other brand settings.

Who is this best for?

Small businesses with 5 to 250 users that need repeatable training, consistent service delivery, mobile access to procedures, and better control over operational knowledge.
